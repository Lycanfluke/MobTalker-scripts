Installation instructions
==========================

To install, first make sure you have the MobTalker Mod correctly installed.
Then just drop the "mobTalker_script" folder into your minecraft resources folder.



Installations-Anleitung
==========================

Zum installieren, stelle erst sicher, dass der Mobtalker-Mod korrekt installiert wurde.
